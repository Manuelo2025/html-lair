The Early Years

The arts called me right from my early days; yeah, as early as I could remember.
From composing music and songs that never existed to writing poems and short stories, I was always in love with the arts.
And then it went to singing in a language I never knew, or more like, never existed. Ha! I was creating my own language, even though, I never really did think it that way then.
I was just having fun, and I loved it. I still do!

My Journey

As I grew older, I started to take my passion for the arts more seriously. I began to study music theory and composition, and I started to write my own songs and poems.
I also started to explore other forms of art, such as painting and photography.
I found that the arts were a way for me to express myself and to connect with others in a deeper way.
I was able to share my thoughts and feelings with the world, and I found that this was a powerful way to communicate.
